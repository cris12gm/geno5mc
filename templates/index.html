geno5mC

geno5mC is a database and web-server to explore the associations between DNA methylation and the genotype. Using 58 publically available whole genome bisulfite sequencing datasets we determine sequence variation and methylation state at a single cytosine resolution by means of MethylExtract2. Statistically significant associations (FDR <= 0.1) are determined by means of a Fisher Exact test.
The database can be explored in different ways, either by querying one or several SNP IDs or by means of a gene name.
